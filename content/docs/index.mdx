This digital garden is my personal repository of knowledge, that I update from time to time when I learn something interesting or research a topic. Generally speaking, it will capture thoughts I have about things I do on a daily basis, in particular building digital products.

The site's source code is available at [https://www.github.com/artofrawr/garden](https://www.github.com/artofrawr/garden) and is built using the excellent [Fumadocs](https://fumadocs.vercel.app/), using Next.js, App Router, MDX and Tailwind CSS under the hood. 

## What is a Digital Garden?

A digital garden is an approach to publishing personal knowledge on the web. Different from a blog that contains polished articles in reverse chronological order, digital gardens are more like work-in-progress wikis. A collection of unrefined, incomplete ideas that grow and evolve over time through constant tending (editing, rewriting and revising).
